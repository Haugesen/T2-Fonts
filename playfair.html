Playfair Display

— By Claus Sørensen —

Fontdesigner: Claus Eggers Sørensen

Introduction.

Playfair Display is an open-source, Transitional serif typeface designed by Danish type designer Claus Eggers Sørensen in 2011. The design is influenced by typefaces from the mid to late eighteenth century, such as Baskerville. As the name indicates, Playfair Display is well suited for titling and headlines, however, for long stretches of body copy the delicate, high-contrast strokes might hinder readability, especially when used at smaller sizes.

Playfair Display uses the Open Font Lisence.
